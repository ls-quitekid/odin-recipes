Chapathi

Description

Incredients

  • Wheat flour - 1/2 KG
  • Oil
  • Salt
  • Gee
  • Chapathi maker

Steps:

  1. Get some wheat flour in a vessal and put needed amount of salt and using water mix thme until the flour didn't stick to hand.
  2. Leave it like 2 to 3 hours to marinate.
  3. After 2 to 3 hours make small rounds out of the flour.
  4. Using the chapathi maker spread the small round into big rounds.
  5. Heat it until it became golden brown color.
