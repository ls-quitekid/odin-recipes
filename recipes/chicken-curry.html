Chicken Curry

Description

Incredients

  • Chichken - 1/2 KG
  • Chicken Masala - 50 Gram
  • Oil
  • Salt
  • Onion - 2
  • Tomato - 2
  • Corriender Leaves
  • Garlic Paste

Steps:

  1. Chop the onion and tomato into small pieces and keep them ready.
  2. Clean the chicken and mix salt, Garlic paste and Chicken masala and keep it for 15 minutes to marinate.
  3. Put your vassel in the stove and take 50 ml oil and put onion and tomato.
  4. Once the onion and tomato are squiced put the marinated chicken and mix them.
  5. Add some water and keep the flame in low for 15 minutes.
